OVERVIEW

After School is a free interactive short story.
It was developed in 2 days, during a masterclass with Game Art students
at ECV Aquitaine.

Characters and backgrounds are hand drawn, painted with watercolors and animated on paper frame-by-frame.

>>>> PLAY THE GAME BY FOLLOWING THIS LINK



Developer: Atelier Sentô and students at ECV Aquitaine (AM1-AM2 Game)2015-2016
Genre: point-and-click
Software: Unity


STORY

Every evening, as she walks back from school, a Japanese girl
stops at the window of the boy she loves in secret.
But he only has eyes for his books and doesn't notice her.
The girl dreams of the day she will find the strength to talk to him.
